Privacy

Plain statement.

Split Check is designed to be private by default. All data stays on your device. Nothing is transmitted, stored remotely, or shared with third parties.

Last updated · 29 May 2026

Scope

What data the app handles

The app only touches data you create or capture inside it.

  • Saved splits (bill, tip %, people count, computed totals, timestamp).

Storage

How that data is stored

Everything lives in AsyncStorage on your device. Only the most recent 10 saved splits are retained.

  • Deleting items from inside the app removes them permanently from your device.
  • Uninstalling the app removes all stored data.

Negative space

What is NOT collected or transmitted

It's worth being explicit about the absences, since those are the parts privacy policies usually elide.

  • No analytics or telemetry of any kind.
  • No advertising identifiers.
  • No third-party SDKs that collect data.
  • No account, no login, no user profile.
  • No network requests for your data — nothing leaves your device.

Children

Children's privacy

Split Check is a general-purpose utility and is not directed at children under 13. It does not knowingly collect data that could identify a user of any age — because it does not collect or transmit personal data at all.

Versioning

Changes to this policy

If material changes are made, the last updated date at the top of this page will change. Continued use of the app after an update constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
