Features

What's in the build

The whole feature set, plainly listed — no upsells gating the basics.

$

Large amount entry

Big focused bill input with auto-decimal-pad. Comma or dot both work as the decimal separator.

%

Tip presets + custom

10 · 15 · 18 · 20 · 25 — the common US restaurant brackets. Type in a custom percent for anywhere else.

⊞

Party-size stepper

Tap − / + to change how many ways the bill splits. The per-person amount updates live in big type.

⤓

Copy summary

Drops the bill / tip / per-person breakdown on your clipboard for a quick paste into your group chat.

◧

Recent splits

Save a calculation to the Recent list. Tap any past split to restore those values.

⊘

Nothing tracked

No analytics, no signup, no network requests. Everything is local arithmetic.

How to play

From cold-start to good-at-it

A few short steps. The app gets out of your way after that.

01

Type the bill

Open the app, type the amount in the big input. Decimal pad opens automatically.

02

Pick a tip

Tap a preset percent or type a custom one. The total updates as you change it.

03

Set people

Use the − / + stepper to set how many ways the bill splits. Each pays appears below.

04

Share or save

Tap Copy summary to paste the breakdown into a message, or Save to keep it in Recent.
