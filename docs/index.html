Features

What's in the build

The whole feature set, plainly listed — no upsells gating the basics.

$

Big-thumb input

Bill amount lives in a single oversized field at the top. Decimal-pad keyboard, dot or comma accepted (locale-tolerant).

%

Preset chips + custom

10 / 15 / 18 / 20 / 25 percent presets in one tap. Tap Custom to type any percentage. Negative values clamp to 0.

▣

Party stepper

Plus / minus stepper for the number of people, plus a direct entry field for big tables. Minimum 1.

✶

Per-person breakdown

Total, tip amount, and per-person share, all shown together. The per-person amount is the headline.

⤓

Copy summary

Tap Copy to put a clean text summary on your clipboard — perfect for posting in a group chat at the end of dinner.

⌂

Recent splits

The last 10 splits you tap Save on are remembered. Tap any recent row to restore those values; tap × to delete.

How to play

From cold-start to good-at-it

A few short steps. The app gets out of your way after that.

01

Type the bill

Big number field at the top. Decimal-pad input — '47.50' or '47,50' both work.

02

Pick a tip

Tap one of the preset chips, or type a custom percent. Tapping a preset clears the custom input.

03

Set the party size

Use the +/- stepper or tap the number to type a value. Minimum is 1.

04

Read the result

Per-person amount is the big number in the middle. Tip and total are listed above it; per-person tip and per-person bill are below.

05

Save or copy

Tap Save to store the split in Recent (last 10 kept). Tap Copy to put a clean text summary on your clipboard.
