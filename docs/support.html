Support

Frequently asked, honestly answered.

If your question isn't here, open an issue on GitHub. Public, traceable, and the same channel I use for everything else.

Why don't I see negative amounts?

Negative inputs are clamped to 0. A negative bill or negative tip would produce nonsense numbers; the app refuses them silently rather than displaying broken math.

Does the app support other currencies?

The display uses US dollar formatting ($) right now. The underlying math is currency-agnostic; a future version will detect device locale and switch the symbol automatically.

How long are saved splits kept?

Only the last 10 splits you explicitly tap Save on. Newer saves push the oldest off. Tap × on any row to delete a single split.

Is anything sent anywhere?

No. Bills, splits, and history all live on this device only, in AsyncStorage. No analytics, no third-party SDKs.

What does Copy put on the clipboard?

A short summary like 'Bill $47.50 · 20.0% tip ($9.50) · 4 people · Total $57.00 · Each $14.25' — paste it into any chat app.

Get in touch

Need help, or want to report a bug?

Open an issue on GitHub. There's no support email — issues are the canonical channel and they're public, which keeps everything honest.
